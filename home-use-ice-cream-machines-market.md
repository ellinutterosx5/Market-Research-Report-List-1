Soft ice cream machines create a creamy, smooth texture, often used for soft-serve ice cream, requiring minimal freezing time. In contrast, hard ice cream machines churn ice cream to achieve a denser consistency, suitable for traditional ice cream flavors and requiring longer freezing periods. Both types cater to different preferences, allowing consumers to enjoy homemade ice cream according to their desired texture and style.</p></p>
