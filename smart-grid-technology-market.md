<p><ul><li>Distribution Management Systems (DMS)</li><li>Demand Response Management Systems (DRM)</li><li>Meter Data Management Systems (MDMS)</li><li>Supervisory Control and Data Acquisition (SCADA)</li><li>Outage Management Systems (OMS)</li><li>Smart Meter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Smart Grid Technology encompasses various systems enhancing energy efficiency and reliability. Distribution Management Systems (DMS) optimize the distribution network. Demand Response Management Systems (DRM) adjust consumer energy usage during peak periods. Meter Data Management Systems (MDMS) efficiently collect and analyze consumption data. Supervisory Control and Data Acquisition (SCADA) enables real-time monitoring and control of infrastructure. Outage Management Systems (OMS) improve outage response times. The Smart Meter market focuses on advanced metering technologies providing detailed consumption insights, facilitating better energy management.</p></p>
