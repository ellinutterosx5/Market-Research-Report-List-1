<p><p>The coil coating additives market includes two main types: solventborne and waterborne. Solventborne additives utilize organic solvents for application, offering advantages like improved flow and leveling characteristics but raising environmental concerns due to volatile organic compounds (VOCs). In contrast, waterborne additives use water as the primary solvent, making them more environmentally friendly with lower VOC emissions. Waterborne systems often provide easier cleanup and safer handling, driving their increasing adoption in environmentally conscious industries.</p></p>
