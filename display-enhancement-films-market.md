<p><ul><li>Passive Enhancement</li><li>Active Enhancement</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Display enhancement films are categorized into two main types: passive enhancement and active enhancement. Passive enhancement films improve display quality by increasing brightness, contrast, and viewing angles without external power. They utilize optical techniques like diffusion and reflection. In contrast, active enhancement films require energy to function, often incorporating technologies like liquid crystals or electrochromic materials to dynamically adjust display characteristics. Both types aim to optimize visual performance in various devices, enhancing user experience across applications.</p></p>
