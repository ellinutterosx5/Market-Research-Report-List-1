<p><ul><li>Flat-Flat</li><li>Round-Flat</li><li>Round-Round</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The hot stamping machine market is categorized into three main types based on the configuration of the stamping surfaces. Flat-Flat machines feature flat dies that produce designs on flat substrates, making them suitable for various applications. Round-Flat machines use round dies to create designs on flat surfaces, ideal for items like labels. Round-Round machines utilize both round dies on round substrates, often employed in decorative packaging and other cylindrical products. Each type serves specific production needs and enhances customization options.</p></p>
