<p><ul><li>CR Tech Digital X-Ray System</li><li>DR Tech Digital X-Ray System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Pediatric Digital X-ray System market is primarily divided into two types: CR (Computed Radiography) Tech Digital X-ray Systems and DR (Digital Radiography) Tech Digital X-ray Systems. CR Tech systems utilize a phosphor imaging plate to capture images, which are then digitally processed, making them more cost-effective and suitable for facilities transitioning from traditional X-ray. In contrast, DR Tech systems use flat-panel detectors for immediate image acquisition and superior image quality, enhancing diagnostic accuracy and workflow efficiency. Both technologies cater specifically to pediatric patients, ensuring optimized radiation doses and tailored imaging solutions.</p></p>
