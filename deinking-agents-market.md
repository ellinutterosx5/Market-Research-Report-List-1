<p><p>The deinking agents market comprises various types essential for recycling paper. Fatty alcohol-based deinking agents effectively remove ink due to their surfactant properties, enhancing ink detachment from fibers. Foaming agents help create bubbles that trap and lift ink particles, improving the flotation process. Collecting agents work by agglomerating ink particles, facilitating their removal during the recycling process. Additionally, other agents may include enzymes or biocatalysts that assist in breaking down ink components, further enhancing deinking efficiency.</p></p>
