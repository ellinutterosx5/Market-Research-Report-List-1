<p><ul><li>Gaussian Beam EBL Systems</li><li>Shaped Beam EBL Systems</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Multi Technique Electron Beam Lithography (EBL) systems market features two primary types: Gaussian Beam EBL Systems and Shaped Beam EBL Systems. Gaussian Beam EBL Systems utilize a focused Gaussian beam for high-resolution patterning, offering precision for fine structures in semiconductor manufacturing and research. In contrast, Shaped Beam EBL Systems employ a shaped electron beam, enabling faster writing speeds and improved throughput for larger areas or simpler designs. Both technologies cater to different applications, enhancing the versatility and efficiency of EBL processes.</p></p>
