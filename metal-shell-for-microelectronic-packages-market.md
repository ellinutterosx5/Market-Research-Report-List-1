<p><ul><li>TO Shell</li><li>Flat Shell</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Metal Shells for microelectronic packages primarily fall into two categories: TO (Transistor Outline) shells and Flat shells. TO shells are cylindrical and designed for housing semiconductor devices, providing rugged protection while ensuring efficient thermal management. They are commonly used in applications requiring reliable performance in compact spaces. Flat shells, on the other hand, are typically used for surface-mounted devices, offering a low-profile design that facilitates easy integration into electronic circuits and enhances overall space efficiency. Both types ensure durability and functionality in microelectronic applications.</p></p>
