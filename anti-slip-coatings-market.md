<p><p>The Anti-Slip Coatings market is primarily divided into two types: water-based and solvent-based coatings. Water-based anti-slip coatings are eco-friendly, easy to apply, and have low VOC emissions, making them ideal for indoor environments. Solvent-based coatings offer stronger adhesion and durability, suitable for high-traffic or outdoor applications. Each type caters to specific needs; water-based coatings are often preferred for residential use, while solvent-based options are favored in industrial and commercial settings for their robustness and performance under harsh conditions.</p></p>
