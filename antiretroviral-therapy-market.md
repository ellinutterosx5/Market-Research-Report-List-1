<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Antiretroviral Therapy Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>NNRTIs</li><li>NRTI</li><li>Multiclass Combination Products</li><li>Integrase Inhibitors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The antiretroviral therapy market includes several key types: </p><p>1. **NRTIs (Nucleoside Reverse Transcriptase Inhibitors)** block reverse transcriptase, preventing HIV replication.</p><p>2. **NNRTIs (Non-Nucleoside Reverse Transcriptase Inhibitors)** also inhibit reverse transcriptase but through a different mechanism, offering distinct treatment options. </p><p>3. **Integrase Inhibitors** target the integrase enzyme, crucial for viral DNA integration into the host genome.</p><p>4. **Multiclass Combination Products** combine these classes to enhance efficacy, simplify regimens, and improve patient adherence in HIV treatment.</p></p>
